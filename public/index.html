About Red Dirt Mentoring

Red Dirt Mentoring was born out of the trials of COVID-19. As learning rapidly moved online, it presented a unique opportunity; geographical location was no longer a barrier to tutoring! Quickly, our tutors reached out to schools across the country and now provide tutoring to disadvantaged students, regardless of location, because we believe a student’s future should not be restricted by where they go to school.
